Mile 02 — The van

A real salon. Really parked outside.

Custom-built in 2026 on a long-wheelbase Sprinter. It never plugs into your house — everything it needs, it carries.

  1. Backwash basin

    Full shampoo bowl with on-board hot water. Yes, a real rinse in your driveway.

  2. Hydraulic chair

    The same chair your favorite salon has, bolted to a van floor with a view of your street.

  3. Full color bar

    60+ shades of professional color, developers, and bond builders mixed on board.

  4. Daylight-true station

    Color-balanced lighting around the mirror, so your toner reads the same indoors and out.

  5. Hot towels, always

    Warm towels, cold drinks, good playlists. The small stuff is the point.

  6. AC rated for July

    Dual-zone climate built for Phoenix summers. 75°F inside when it’s 112°F outside.

7 — underneath it all: fresh-water and grey-water tanks plus whisper-quiet battery power. We never run a cord to your house or a hose to your spigot.
